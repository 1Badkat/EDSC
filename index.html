Welcome to the Eganville & District Sportsman's Club

We are a community-focused club offering a variety of outdoor and shooting sports activities. Our facilities include rifle and handgun ranges, trap shooting, archery, and more. We are committed to wildlife conservation and community involvement.

Join us for events, training courses, and recreational activities throughout the year.
