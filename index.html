About Us

Founded in 1978, the Eganville & District Sportsman's Club (EDSC) is located near the Bonnechere Caves and owns 400 acres of land. The club promotes conservation, safe hunting practices, and community involvement.

Facilities

  • Handgun ranges: 15, 25, 50 meters
  • Rifle ranges: 50 to 400 meters
  • Shotgun range with automatic clays thrower
  • Archery range
  • Clubhouse
  • Wildlife conservation areas

Events

  • Monthly Trap Shoots
  • Annual Gun Show (largest in Ontario)
  • Action Handgun Shoots
  • Cowboy Action Shoot
  • Running/Standing Deer Competition

Firearm Courses

We offer Hunter Education and Firearms Safety Courses. Registration is available at http://www.ontariohunt.ca.

Membership

Membership includes:

  • OFAH membership
  • Access to ranges and events
  • $5M liability insurance
  • Subscription to Ontario Out of Doors Magazine

Conservation Projects

  • Wild turkey introduction
  • Walleye stocking and habitat improvement
  • Wood duck nesting boxes
  • Shrub planting for wildlife
